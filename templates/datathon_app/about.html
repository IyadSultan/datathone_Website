About the Event

A week-long journey of innovation, learning, and cultural exchange in healthcare data science.

Event Overview

The KHCC Datathon & Workshop brings together healthcare professionals, data scientists, and researchers to explore innovative solutions using the MIMIC database.

This unique event combines hands-on learning, real-world problem-solving, and cultural experiences in the heart of Jordan.

The Datathon

  • MIMIC Database Access
    Work with real healthcare data in a secure environment
  • Team Configuration
    Collaborate in diverse teams of 3-5 members
  • Infrastructure
    Access to high-performance computing resources

The Workshop

  • Expert Sessions
    Learn from MIT faculty and industry leaders
  • Hands-on Training
    Practical sessions on data analysis tools
  • Certification
    Receive official recognition of participation
